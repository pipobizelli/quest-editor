# HeroQuest — Spells Reference

> All spells for the quest-editor LLM agents. Based on custom Spell Magic Memory cards.

---

## Spell Rules

- **Wizard**: Chooses 3 of the 4 Hero spell groups (9 spells total).
- **Elf**: Chooses 1 Hero spell group (3 spells total).
- Each spell can be cast **once per Quest**. After casting, the card is discarded until the next Quest.
- Casting a spell counts as the Hero's **action** for that turn.
- Spells require **Line of Sight** to the target (unless stated otherwise).
- Between Quests, all spells are restored.
- A monster can only be targeted by a spell the caster can "see" (Line of Sight).
- **Important**: You may only cast a spell on your turn. A spell may be cast instead of attacking.

## Hero Spells

### Air Spells

#### Genie
- **Type**: Ritual
- *This ritual calls forth a powerful spirit to do your bidding.*
- **Effect 1**: Open one Door anywhere on the board, revealing what lies beyond.
- **Effect 2**: Target figure within Line of Sight is attacked with 6 combat dice.

#### Swift Wind
- **Type**: Cantrip
- *This cantrip lends vigour to the limbs.*
- **Effect 1**: Target figure within Line of Sight gains **Haste 1** during their next activation.
- **Effect 2**: The caster gains **Haste 1** immediately.

#### Tempest
- **Type**: Invocation
- *This invocation creates a small whirlwind that will envelop the enemy.*
- **Effect**: Target figure in Line of Sight must miss their next Activation.

---

### Earth Spells

#### Heal Body
- **Type**: Ritual
- *This ritual will restore the flesh.*
- **Effect**: One target figure within Line of Sight heals up to 4 lost Body Points.

#### Pass Through Rock
- **Type**: Cantrip
- *This cantrip allows easy passage through the underworld.*
- **Effect**: Target figure may ignore all Furniture, Walls, Blocked Squares, and Rubble counters the next time they take a Move Action.

#### Rock Skin
- **Type**: Invocation
- *This invocation toughens the body against the rigours of the world.*
- **Effect**: One target figure in Line of Sight gains +1 Defend Die permanently. This lasts until the target suffers any Body Point damage.

---

### Fire Spells

#### Ball of Flame
- **Type**: Invocation
- *This invocation will envelope a foe in fire.*
- **Effect**: Attack with combat dice. For this attack, treat enemy shields as Skulls (they count as hits instead of blocks).

#### Courage
- **Type**: Ritual
- *This Ritual instils unswerving bravery in a warrior's heart.*
- **Effect**: One target figure in Line of Sight gains extra Attack + 2 Defend dice rolls. This lasts until there are no enemies in Line of Sight of the target.

#### Fire of Wrath
- **Type**: Cantrip
- *This cantrip will blast an enemy with flame.*
- **Effect**: One figure **anywhere on the game board** is hit with combat dice + fire. For this attack, treat friendly shields as Skulls.

---

### Water Spells

#### Sleep
- **Type**: Invocation
- *This invocation places an enemy into a dreamless slumber.*
- **Effect**: Target figure may not take any Actions and rolls no dice in defence. At the start of their turn, they may roll a number of Defend Dice equal to their current Mind Points. If they roll any friendly shields, the effect ends.
- **Note**: Ineffective against Undead (Mind 0).

#### Veil of Mist
- **Type**: Cantrip
- *This cantrip allows the foe to be evaded.*
- **Effect**: One target figure within Line of Sight may, during their next Movement Action, treat enemy figures as friends (pass through them freely).

#### Water of Healing
- **Type**: Ritual
- *This ritual will revitalise the weary.*
- **Effect**: One target figure within Line of Sight heals up to 4 lost Body Points.

## Chaos Spells (Zargon's Monsters)

Chaos Spells are used by Zargon's special monsters as noted in Quest Notes. There are 12 Chaos Spells total.

### Air Elemental Chaos

#### Cloud of Chaos
- *Smoke, light, and sound pour forth from the sorcerer's hands, filling the world with confusion.*
- **Effect**: Target a room within Line of Sight. All figures in that room are affected. Affected figures may not take any Actions when Activated. At the end of their Activation, affected figures must roll a number of red dice equal to their Mind Points. If they roll any sixes, the effect ends for that figure.

#### Hurricane
- *The sorcerer whistles up a wind to carry his enemies away.*
- **Effect**: One target figure in Line of Sight is moved, in a straight line, directly away from the caster, until they reach a wall, closed door, other blockage, or trap (which they will set off).

#### Lightning Bolt
- *Energy springs from the hands of the sorcerer, scorching all in its path.*
- **Effect**: Target a straight line of spaces (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) directly away from the caster, until it reaches a wall, closed door, or other blockage. All figures in the target area lose 2 Body Points.

#### Summon Orcs
- *The sorcerer summons a number of Orcs to battle.*
- **Effect**: Roll 1 red die to determine how many Orcs arrive. Place them as close to the caster as possible. Summoned figures may be Activated this turn.
  - 1-3 = Four Orcs
  - 4-5 = Five Orcs
  - 6 = Six Orcs

#### Summon Undead
- *The sorcerer summons the fallen back to life.*
- **Effect**: Roll 1 red die to determine what figures arrive. Place them as close to the caster as possible. Summoned figures may be activated this turn.
  - 1-2 = Four Skeletons
  - 3-4 = Three Skeletons, Two Zombies
  - 5-6 = Two Zombies, Two Mummies

### Fire Elemental Chaos

#### Conflagration
- *The sorcerer summons a blazing orb of destruction.*
- **Effect**: Attack with combat dice. For this attack, treat friendly shields as Skulls.

#### Firestorm
- *The sorcerer fills the dungeon with roiling magical fire.*
- **Effect**: Target all figures in the same room as the Caster. All figures in the target room (save for the caster) are hit with combat dice. For this attack, treat enemy shields as Skulls.

### Water Elemental Chaos

#### Rust
- *Calling forth water and driving out fire, the sorcerer rusts metal to nothingness.*
- **Effect**: One Card with the **Metal** Keyword carried by a target figure in Line of Sight is destroyed and discarded.

#### Torpor
- *The Sorcerer lulls the enemy into a deep, deep sleep.*
- **Effect**: Target figure may not take any Actions and rolls no dice in defence. At the start of their turn, they may roll a number of Defend Dice equal to their current Mind Points. If they roll any friendly shields, this effect ends.

### Control Philosophic Chaos

#### Command
- *The sorcerer attempts to control the mind of a hero.*
- **Effect**: One target figure within Line of Sight of the caster is treated as belonging to the same player that controls the caster from now on. At the start of each of their future turns, the figure's rightful controller rolls red dice equal to the target's current Mind Points. If any sixes are rolled, the effect ends.

#### Fear
- *The sorcerer conjures images of horror to drive away those whose resolve is weak.*
- **Effect**: One target figure in Line of Sight rolls only 1 Defend Die (after modification) for all attacks from now on. At the start of their turn, they may roll a number of red dice equal to their current Mind Points. If they roll any sixes, this effect ends.

### Protection Philosophic Chaos

#### Escape
- *The sorcerer makes to evade an overly powerful enemy.*
- **Effect**: Remove the caster from the game board. They are replaced in a position determined by the Quest Book. If this area is yet to be explored, they are not placed on the board until it is found.
